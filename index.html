About Ondo State

History

Ondo State is located in the western part of Nigeria. It is bounded by Kwara and Kogi State on the North Edo State on the East, Delta state on the South-East Osun and Ogun State on the West and by the Bight of Benin at the Atlantic Ocean on the South.

Ondo State includes mangrove-swamp forest near the Bight of Benin, tropical rain forest in the centre part, and wooded savanna on the gentle slopes of the Yoruba Hills in the North.

Agricultural Enrichment

The major agricultural produce found in Ondo State are:

  1. Cotton
  2. Tobacco
  3. Cocoa
  4. Rubber
  5. Timber
  6. Palm Kernel and Oil

Food

Ondo State is known for her variety of delicacies such as:

  1. Ikokore
  2. Pounded Yam and Efo riro
  3. Ishapa
  4. Obe Eja tutu
  5. Efo Amunututu
  6. Boli (Grilled Plantain)
